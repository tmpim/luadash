Operators
=========

As a convenience, Luadash provides named functions
for all of Lua's operators.

* _.ops.plus corresponds to +
* _.ops.minues corresponds to -
* _.ops.times corresponds to *
* _.ops.over corresponds to /
* _.ops.power corresponds to ^
* _.ops.modulo corresponds to %
* _.ops.remainder corresponds to %
* _.ops.rem corresponds to %
* _.ops.mod corresponds to %
* _.ops.conj corresponds to and
* _.ops.disj corresponds to or
* _.ops.equals corresponds to ==

Additionally, _.ops.divisible_by(x, y) tests to see
if y is divisible by x.
